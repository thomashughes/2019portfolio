Canine Cuddles

Canine Cuddles started as a dog walking business based in the heart of Staffordshire. Over time, I've built the website up from a simple one-page site to a multifunctional, powerful e-commerce site. Utilizing the magic power of Wordpress and its many different and powerful plugins, customers can order products and get them delivered straight to their door, without me having to do much at all.
